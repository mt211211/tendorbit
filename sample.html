Why it may fit

  • The DPS explicitly covers passenger lifts, vertical lifts, stairlifts and hoists.
  • Applications can be made during the open DPS period rather than through a single short response window.
  • The notice states that it is suitable for SMEs and available across the UK public sector.

What must be checked before proceeding

  • Which of the four service categories the contractor can evidence.
  • Geographic mobilisation and response-time capability.
  • Required insurance, quality, safety and engineer-competence evidence in the linked tender pack.
  • Commercial terms, call-off mechanics and any minimum capacity requirement.

Provisional decision

Investigate. The public notice supplies enough positive evidence to justify opening the tender pack, but not enough to recommend applying without comparing its mandatory requirements to the contractor's evidence vault.
