Questions, answered.

We keep the commercial promise deliberately narrow: better opportunity intelligence, grounded in source documents.

Does Tendorbit submit bids for us?

No. Tendorbit prepares intelligence and draft support. Your business verifies every fact and controls every submission.

Do you guarantee we will win?

No. Procurement outcomes depend on your capabilities, evidence, pricing and the buyer's evaluation. We help you spend attention on better-fit opportunities.

Where do opportunities come from?

We begin with official UK public procurement sources including Contracts Finder and Find a Tender, then link every brief back to its source.

What information is needed?

Your public website, services, coverage, preferred contract size and a checklist of relevant certificates or policies. Do not email confidential bid material for the free sample.
